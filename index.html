Flip 7 Card Game Rules

Flip 7 is a press-your-luck card game where players race to be the first to reach 200 points. Learn the complete Flip 7 rules: draw cards, avoid duplicates to prevent busting, and use the Flip 7 bonus by collecting 7 unique number cards for 15 extra points. Master Flip 7 scoring rules with our multiplayer score tracker and calculator.
